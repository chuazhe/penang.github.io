Penang

Info

Penang is a Malaysian state located on the northwest coast of Peninsular Malaysia, by the Malacca Strait.

Geography

It has two parts: Penang Island, where the capital city, George Town, is located, and Seberang Perai (formerly Province Wellesley) on the Malay Peninsula. The second smallest Malaysian state by land mass, Penang is bordered by Kedah to the north and the east, and Perak to the south.
